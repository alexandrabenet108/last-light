Carama-śloka
one of the rahasya-traya-mantras
The initiated devotee should chant the following two mantras before going to sleep at night.
Bhagavad Gītā · 18.66
sarva dharmān parityajya
māṁ ekaṁ śaraṇaṁ vraja |
ahaṁ tvā sarva-pāpebhyo
mokṣayiṣyāmi mā śucaḥ ॥ 66 ॥
Abandon all dharmas and take refuge in Me alone. I will deliver you from all sin and evil, do not grieve.
Prayer of Self-offering
at the close of the day
At the end of the day, offer everything to Lord Kṛṣṇa:
śrī kṛṣṇa-arpaṇam astu
'Lord Kṛṣṇa says that whatever you do in life — breathing, your sādhana, eating, drinking, sitting or listening — make it an offering. Make it as an act of surrender to the Lord. Turn it into a prayer to the Lord and offer the prayer to Him. Offer Him the merits of everything you do. You will attain Him and He, who is not attainable by any yogī, will surrender Himself to you.'
— Paramahamsa Vishwananda
Śrī Kṛṣṇa Arpaṇam Astu
offered at the Lotus Feet of Sri Hari
